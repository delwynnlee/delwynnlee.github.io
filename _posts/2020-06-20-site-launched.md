Well. Finally got around to putting this old website together. Neat thing about it - powered by [Jekyll](http://jekyllrb.com) and I can use Markdown to author my posts. It actually is a lot easier than I thought it was going to be.

The last time I wrote on a consistent basis was back when I was in Junior College. That was also the time when Blogspot was the in thing, pre-Friendster, pre-Facebook. Now, that was more than 15 years ago.

Forgive me if my writing is atrocious in these initial posts. My daily form of practice comes from emails and Python codes, hardly the optimum avenue for practising good writing.

Since I am starting this blog series again, I thought I should outline what it will probably talk about. Since I am still in the middle of a career revamp into data analytics and techy stuff, you can expect many posts about me figuring out how things work and on mini projects. I am also into specialty coffee brewing. I also have interests in food, Asian countries, scifi and fantasy, so you may find a few reviews and discussions here and there. 

With such a mish mash of topics, what am I hoping to achieve? Firstly, to cultivate and renew my interest in writing. Secondly, to write good enough posts so I can enjoy reading them back a decade later. 

In this age of social media, I have no expectations that anyone will seriously read these posts. Nonetheless, I will keep these posts non-contentious so to avoid any hassle.
